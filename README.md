The open-source backend behind **Iter Maps** — public-transport journey planning
and maps for Europe. It turns open data into routing, search, live transit, and
the map.

### What it does

- **Journey planning** — A→B across metro, bus, tram, rail, and walking, with live delays.
- **Search & places** — address and place search, with rich details and photos.
- **Maps & overlays** — a vector basemap and styles, with transit lines and stations on top.
- **Offline & live** — pre-download an area for offline use; live arrivals and train boards.
