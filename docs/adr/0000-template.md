# NNNN — Short decision title

- **Status:** Proposed
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Supersedes:** — (or NNNN)
- **Superseded by:** — (filled in if this is ever replaced)

## Context

The forces at play: the problem, the constraints, the relevant invariants, and
anything a future reader needs to understand *why this was even a decision*.
State facts, not the conclusion.

## Decision

What we decided, in the active voice: "We will …". Be specific enough that
someone could act on it.

## Consequences

What becomes true as a result — the good, the bad, and the follow-on work it
forces. Name what gets harder, not just what gets easier.

## Alternatives considered

Each realistic option and the one-line reason it lost. An ADR with no
alternatives usually means the decision wasn't really made.
